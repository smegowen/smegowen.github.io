About Me

Professional background in health and fitness (as a personal trainer and sports performance coach) and sports coaching (mostly tennis). I have also worked in renewable energy at a residential solar company and in consumer packaged goods at a drink startup in the cannabis industry. I recently moved to Hawaii where I will continue to coach tennis and work in the health and fitness industry. I am also interested in sustainable agriculture and land use economics. In my free time I enjoy hiking, trail running, swimming, surfing, paddling, diving, and spear fishing. I am learning full stack web development so that I can design and build websites related to my work and my interests.
